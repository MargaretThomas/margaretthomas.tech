About Me

Originally from Durban, South Africa, I stepped in the world of software development when I was in high school. I enjoy the art of creation as well as puzzle solving - creating software allows me to enjoy both. I went on to further my studies in software development, graduating with distinctions.

I began my professional journey at immedia, a software development studio based in Durban. Thereafter, I moved to Cape Town to join the Flash herd.

Over the years, I have mostly specialised in the Microsoft world although I do enjoy building solutions with other tech stacks. My passion lies in the SDLC, from the conception of projects, the puzzle solving and conversations with a duck, to iteration and maintenance plans. As such, I lean towards language agnostic thinking - by looking at a specific project, planning out the functionality and implementing using a tech stack that is most suited to the team, hosting requirements and project requirements.

I really enjoy taking an iterative approach to building software and in my career as a whole. It allows for growth, collaboration, optimisation and clear alignment with the needs of end users.
